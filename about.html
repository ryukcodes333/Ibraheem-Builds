About Me

Who I Am

I'm Ibraheem, a 16-year-old self-taught developer.

I didn't learn this stuff in a classroom. I started by messing around with WhatsApp and Telegram bots because I wanted to automate things, then somehow ended up learning APIs, databases, backends, and AI.

Most of what I know came from breaking things, searching errors at 2AM, and trying again.

I build whatever seems interesting, share the process publicly, and learn something new with every project.
